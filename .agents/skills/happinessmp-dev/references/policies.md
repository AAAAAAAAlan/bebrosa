# HappinessMP – Community Guidelines

**Goal:**

These guidelines promote a positive and respectful environment for all players.

## 1. Respect Others

Treat others as you wish to be treated. No insults, discrimination, or provocation.

## 2. Fair Play

No cheating, exploits, or manipulation. Follow both server rules and the HappinessMP ToS.

## 3. Language and Content

No extremist, pornographic, violent, or otherwise unlawful content. Keep chat and voice communication appropriate.

## 4. Advertising and Spam

No advertising without permission; no flooding or spamming messages.

## 5. Reporting Violations

Report rule violations to the server team or via official channels. Abuse of the reporting system is prohibited.

Source: https://happinessmp.net/guidelines

# HappinessMP – Server Operator Policy

**Purpose:**

This policy sets minimum standards for all servers running HappinessMP to ensure a fair, safe, and respectful player experience.

## 1. Compliance with ToS

Servers must comply with the official HappinessMP ToS at all times.

## 2. Age Restriction

Access is limited to players aged 18+. This must be clearly communicated.

## 3. Content and Mods

No illegal content or infringement of third‑party rights; no real‑world brands/logos without permission.

## 4. Player Conduct

Clear rules against cheating, exploits, harassment, discrimination, and spam must be enforced.

## 5. Data Protection

Server operators are responsible for compliance with applicable data protection laws and must inform players about data collection.

## 6. Cooperation

Operators must cooperate with HappinessMP in investigations and implement required changes to protect the Mod’s integrity.
